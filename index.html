York REM Schedule Exporter

Takes York University Registration and Enrolment course details to export into a iCalendar file.

Instructions

  1. Login to REM at https://registrar.yorku.ca/enrol/visiting/enrolment
  2. Select the academic session you'd like to export
  3. Click Continue
  4. Click Course Details
  5. Copy all of your course details
  6. Paste the details into the text box below
  7. Click Export
  8. Import to the calendar you desire or find break times with https://ru3.netlify.app/
